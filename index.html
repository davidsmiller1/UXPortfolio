About

David is a UX designer with an emphasis on social impact. With more than 15 years of professional experience David has worked in local, regional and international business, nonprofit and government organizations. His professional goal is to design products that bring value to the end user and the community.

In his previous roles, he has successfully used concise and reliable written communication, effective project management skills, and cross functional collaboration. He is also an attentive and insightful listener that anticipates the needs of the customer, and is able to synthesize large amounts of information into simple concepts.
